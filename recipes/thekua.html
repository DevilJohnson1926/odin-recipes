Thekua

A traditional deep-fried sweet snack from Bihar, India, often associated with the Chhath Puja festival.

Ingredients for Thekua
  • Wheat Flour
  • Oil
  • Water
  • Sugar
Steps to prepare Thekua
  1. Make syrup of sugar at low temperature
  2. Mix the wheat flour with water to make it dough
  3. Make Thekua with the dough in a proper shape
  4. Then fry it in oil to complete it
