Maggi

Maggi is a fast food which is used to prepare by mothers when their children are in a hurry to leave for school and their breakfast is not ready

Ingredients for Maggi
  • Maggi
  • Water
  • Some Vegetables like pea,tomato,onion etc
Steps to prepare Maggi
  1. Mix all the above mentioned vegetables in a bowl for few minutes
  2. Then add some water into it. After 2 to 3 minutes pour the maggi in the bowl and add masala into the maggi provided by the company
  3. Stir the maggi for few minutes and then leave it covered for few minutes
  4. Then serve it to everyone
