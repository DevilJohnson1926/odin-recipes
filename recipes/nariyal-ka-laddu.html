Nariyal ka Laddu

Nariyal ka Laddu is an Indian dish, used to prepared during festivals, specially during Chhath Pooja. In Chhath Pooja there has been a lot of coconuts available for preparing this dish.

Ingredients Required
  • Fresh Coconuts
  • Coconut Grinder
  • Milk according to Coconuts amount
  • Sugar according to Coconuts
Steps to prepare Laddu
  1. Firstly find fresh coconuts by removing their water
  2. Then wash it and grind it into tiny pieces
  3. Start boiling milk and after milk boiled properly pour the grind part into the milk
  4. Then add sugar accordingly into the milk
  5. After some time when milk absorbed completely then pour it into a plate
  6. Then start making laddu's before the coconuts grind start to become cold.
